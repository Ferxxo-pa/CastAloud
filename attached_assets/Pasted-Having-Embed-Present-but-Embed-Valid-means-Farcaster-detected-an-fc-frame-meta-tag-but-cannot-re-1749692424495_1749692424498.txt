Having Embed Present ✓ but Embed Valid ✕ means Farcaster detected an fc:frame meta tag but cannot render it—likely due to malformed or unreachable image assets in your embed meta or manifest.

🧩 Common Causes & Fixes
1. Splash image issues in farcaster.json manifest

The debug panel often says “Embed Valid ✕” when the splashImageUrl is incorrect.

Make sure it points to a publicly accessible image (200×200px PNG or JPG under 1 MB) with no typos or extra slashes.

2. Frame embed meta tag formatting

In your HTML <head>, the fc:frame tag must use name="fc:frame" (not property="…"), and be valid JSON.

Ensure your preview image uses a 3:2 aspect ratio-sized image (under 10 MB).
